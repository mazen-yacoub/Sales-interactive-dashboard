Sales Interactive Dashboard

A sales analytics dashboard built from AdventureWorks data to explore product performance, customer segments, geography, seasonality, and budget comparison.

$29.36M Total sales analyzed
27.6K Internet sales orders
18.4K Customers represented
96.5% Revenue from bikes

What the dashboard answers

  • Which products and categories generate the most revenue?
  • How do sales trend by year and month?
  • Where are the strongest customer locations?
  • How do customer segments compare?
  • How does sales performance compare with budget?
Built with SQL Server, Power BI, Excel, and GitHub Pages.
